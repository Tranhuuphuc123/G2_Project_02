KAUAI BEACHES
HAWAI (Western United States)

   Hawaii is a state in the Western United States, located in the Pacific Ocean about 2,000 miles from the U.S. mainland. Hawaii consists of countless large and small islands surrounded with clear blue sea water, which is an extremely suitable place for sightseeing, travel and vacation. There are six major islands to visit in Hawaii: Kauai, Oahu, Molokai, Lanai, Maui,.. and the island of Hawaii! kauai is one of them and let's explore it

  Kauai Beach are famous for long stretches of secluded white sand, warm turquoise waters, and tropical backdrops. It is no surprise that Kauai’s beaches are among the top beaches on the planet. The island is surrounded by 50 miles of shimmering white-sand coast where you can enjoy jaw-droppingly beautiful unspoiled views.

  Kauai is home to the only navigable rivers in Hawaii, so kayaking is an integral part of a unique Kauai vacation.

  Most visitors arrive on Kauai at Lihue Airport (LIH) on the island’s East Side. Many airlines offer nonstop service, though some visitors may choose to fly to Honolulu International Airport on Oahu first, then connect to Kauai (depending on times and prices). Ground transportation includes rental cars , taxis and limited bus service. Several companies provide guided tours aboard motor coaches, mini-buses, vans and limousines. If you’re staying at a hotel on Kauai, check to see if airport transportation is offered, as many provide complimentary pick-up and drop-off service. and finally, Let's come to the sea of kauai and enjoy the wonderful beauty of the seas of hawaii
